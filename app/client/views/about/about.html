VHS HACKATHON 2022
About:

A hackathon is an event where people engage in rapid and collaborative engineering over a relatively short period of time
The goal of a hackathon is to create functioning software or hardware by the end of the event and present it.

If you found this, you are probably already in the #hackathon slack channel.
If not please join.
If you need any help finding/forming the team, or help on the day.. or want to bounce your ideas, ask about our current system, talk architecture.. thats a good place to start
Theme of this hackathon:

As this is the companies first hackathon - we are not setting a specific goal or theme. Build whatever you want to build.

Use whatever technology you want to use.
Fix something that makes dev lives better..
a quick win for customers..
an amazing new technology that you think could be cool..
a new product feature.
Why have a hackathon?

A chance to work with people you normally wouldn't.
An opportunity to learn something new.
Space and a time to make headway on problems you are interested in.
A chance to showcase what others may not know is possible.
We believe its important to share learnings. If you participate in the hackathon, we encourage everyone to present.
Even if the demo is not functioning, you likely learned a new technology or learned something that didnt work.
Please present anyway
TBC: We want everyone energized for this hackathon - so we will be organising uber eats vouchers for all participants (will be sent to those who register!)
Rules:

- Max team size 3 people (we only have so many prizes to hand out)
Judging:

A hackathon isn't all about prizes and winning. Work on what makes you interested.. maybe we will find it interesting too.


Qualifying presentation rounds will be run in each region (judged by Jeff and Arnaud, Allan and Ab in APAC)
Final round presentations will judged in 3 categories:
Best potential roadmap project.. what do product think they could turn into something that we should build in the near future? Judged by Facundo
Best shippable project.. what could go live tomorrow and have immediate impact? Judged by Allan
Best technology.. what was built with cool tech that could make a huge impact? Judged by Eugene
